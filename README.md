# 🚗 Green Driver Score: Integrating Eco-Behavior into Insurance Risk Assessment

## 📌 Overview

Green Driver Score is an AI-powered insurance underwriting system that incorporates eco-driving behavior into traditional insurance risk assessment. Using telematics data and machine learning, the system evaluates a driver's driving habits, environmental impact, and safety patterns to generate a comprehensive Green Driver Score.

The objective is to encourage sustainable driving while enabling insurers to make more accurate, transparent, and personalized premium decisions.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Traditional motor insurance underwriting primarily focuses on demographic information, claim history, and vehicle details. It often overlooks real-world driving behavior and environmentally responsible driving practices.

As a result:

* Safe and eco-friendly drivers may not receive fair premium benefits.
* Insurance pricing lacks behavioral personalization.
* Sustainable driving habits are not incentivized.

---

## 💡 Solution

The Green Driver Score platform analyzes driving behavior collected through telematics and vehicle data to generate a sustainability-aware driver score.

The score combines:

* Driving safety metrics
* Eco-driving indicators
* Vehicle usage patterns
* Risk prediction using Machine Learning

This enables insurers to:

* Improve underwriting accuracy
* Reward responsible drivers
* Encourage environmentally friendly driving practices
